🌲 Longleaf Forest School

Longleaf Forest School helped root our family in Durham. We moved from Chicago four years ago, just as our little one was entering preschool, and were fortunate to discover Longleaf. If we were going to live in the Southeast, we wanted to embrace the outdoors — and Longleaf did exactly that. Maddi Sullivan and her team fostered curiosity, empathy, creativity, and kindness every day. From singing about lichen to exploring local wildlife, they helped turn a strange new place into home.

Put the Kettle Away! ☀️

It's Sun Tea season

No need to hassle with a kettle. Sun teas bring back sweet memories of childhood and are a beautiful activity for a play date or a dinner party!

🫙  Combine Kid Clover tea and cold water in a glass jar

☀️  Leave in a sunny spot for 3–4 hours

🧊  Add ice, pour your brew, and rejoice in the sun's beauty & power

Prepared this way, the teas don't release bitter tannins — you get a more delicate, refreshing drink. Summer in a sweet cup!

Made with love by an herbalist mama in Durham, NC
